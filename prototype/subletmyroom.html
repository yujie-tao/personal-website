Sublet My Room

Prototype

SubletMyRoom is a pre-startup proejct trying to solve problem in student subleasing. Partnering with two other students under UNC CS department, we connect students subleasing their rooms to students searching for housing.

This is a class project of my Software Startup class under UNC CS department. Through market research and interviews, we identify housing sublease as a pertinent problem of college students, as there is diconnection of the sublease information flow. There is no centralized platform at UNC that connect the two sides of

We tested our ideas by buildling up a landing page with a sign up form and distributing flyers around the campus. We got 650+ page views on our landing page from from March 20 to April 20, with 80 people responding to our sign up form.

By using the sublease information collected from sign up form, we manually match student renters with student subleasers.

During the whole semester, I:

  • Conducted market research and interviews to understand user needs
  • Determined product functionalities, prototyped in Axure RP and iterated the demo
  • Developed prototype in HTML/CSS, Javascript, Express.js and MongoDB
