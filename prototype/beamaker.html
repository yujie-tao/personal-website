Be A Maker

Prototype
This is a semester-long project for my Introduction to Digital Storytelling class at the School of Media and Journalism of UNC-Chapel Hill. Through learning different media technology skills needed to engage in multimedia storytelling in online environments, I present my primary subject, BeAM Makerspace, by using video, graphics and web. I wish to have more people be aware of the emerging technologies available on campus and inspire them to take a risk, make a difference, be a maker.
