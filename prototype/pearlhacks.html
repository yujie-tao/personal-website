2019 Pearl Hacks

Pearl Hacks is a weekend-long hackathon for female and non-binary college students held at the Univeristy of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. It aims at empowering diversity of technology field.

As the planning director, I recruit and manage 110+ volunteers to serve the hackathon with 700+ registrations. I also lead four members under web development committee in designing and developing a new website to publicize the event.
