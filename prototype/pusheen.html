Pusheen’s Credit Card
Marketplace

Prototype

I participated in Red Ventures Marketplace Build Case Competition in Spring 2018. During competition period, I teamed up with four other students and developed an online marketplace that interacts with a Red Ventures’ API to fetch, display, and market credit cards to consumers. We presented our final product, Pusheen’s Marketplace, to a group of senior engineers at Red Ventures.

Overall architecture:
Front-end: Vue.js,Bootstrap
Back-end: Ruby
Analytics: Google Analytics

I was responsible for UI/UX design and front-end development of the home page.
